SHOP DISPLAY SYSTEMS_

OUR LINE OF DISPLAY PRODUCTS IS DESIGNED FOR SPORTS SHOPS OFFERING PROFESSIONAL SKIING AND SNOWBOARDING EQUIPMENT, CLOTHING, FOOTWEAR, OUTDOOR GEAR AND VARIOUS TYPES OF ACCESSORIES

Each year, we prepare several hundred layouts and visualisations which enable our customers to optimally select their storage systems and make maximum use of the space available in their facilities. using our team's competences and experience, we meticulously develop concepts for floor plans of various sports facilities.

Wall-mounted construction posts with the wall panels are the most important equipment for storing and presenting merchandise in a retail space. The height of the construction posts and wall panels is 2450mm. The width of the panel is 622mm.

The choice of how to fix the posts depends on the construction of the load-bearing walls in the room. The best solution is to fix the construction posts directly into the wall. If the walls are not strong enough, it is necessary to use alternative solutions for stabilizing the display wall panels - an expansion system, or supports

CONSTRUCION FIXING_

WALL FIXING

The choice of fixing depends on the load-bearing parameters of the wall. It is necessary to verify tchem and select solutions that ensure safe use of the products.

EXPANSION SYSTEM

When the load-bearing capacity of the wall is not sufficient, the mounting posts are stabilized using a strut system. Adjustable expansion bolts allow the posts to be attached to the ceiling.

SUPPORTS

When the load-bearing capacity of the wall is not ufficient, the display modules can also be placed against a wall in a free-standing form - on two 540mm long horizontal supports

DOUBLE-SIDED SUPPORTS

Double-sided module supports are used together with double-sided construction posts when the display system is set up in a free-standing form inside a room.

WALL PANELS & DISPLAY ITEMS_

BOARD PANEL

A large selection of types and structures enables imitation of both wood and other materials.

MESH PANEL

Made of wire or expanded steel mesh with different translucency of panels in an RAL colour of your choice.

STEEL PANEL

A modern solid steel surface in an RAL color of your choice.

PIN SYSTEM PANEL

The 120mm-long screw-in pin is part of the footwear support and lateral exposure.

SHOP DISPLAY SYSTEMS_

Once the construction posts system is fixed, a wall panel is placed on the horizontal crossbars to provide a backdrop for the products on display. The back of panels has two upright reinforcing posts with integrated hooks ensuring stability and safety of the display system.

DISPLAY ITEMS ASSEMBLING_

Items dedicated to individual product groups (like skis, snowboards, apparel, footwear, helmets, backpacks, goggles, accessories, etc.) are mounted on the multipurpose crossbars in any configuration by attaching them with small screws tightened from the back.

Thanks to the densely perforated construction posts and the universal fastening system, the arrangement of display items is simple and extremely flexible. Display elements are made of steel with the appropriate thickness and stiffness - adequate to the purpose of the item.
